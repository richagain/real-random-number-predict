How Backtesting Works

  • For each historical draw, we simulate a prediction using only data available before that draw.
  • We then compare the prediction against the actual winning numbers and count matches.
  • Results show how many times each strategy achieved 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6 matches.
  • Higher average matches and hit rates indicate better strategy performance.
